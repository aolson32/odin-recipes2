Oatmeal Cookies

Cookies worth Eating

Cookies are my favorite treat. So easy to grab and go! Oatmeal cookies at least make you feel slightly less bad about eating a bunch of them in one go.

Not that I would ever do such a thing...

Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Old Fashioned Oats
  • Baking Soda
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt
  • Room Temp Unsalted Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Raisins
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Line Baking Sheet with parchment paper
  3. Cream the Butter and Sugar together. Then Add the Egg and Vanilla
  4. In seperate bowl, mix the oats, flour, baking soda, and salt
  5. In the bowl with the sugar mix, add in the flour mix until it is just mixed.
  6. Then mix in the raisins just until they are incorporated.
  7. Scoop the mixture onto the sheets
  8. Bake for 15mins, rotating sheets halfway through
  9. Set Cookies on cooling rack to cool
