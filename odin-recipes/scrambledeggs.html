Scrambled Eggs

Eggs worth Dying For

I dont know why I chose this recipe, I dont even really like eggs that much.

I dont even like bacon or cheese that much either.

But when you mix them all together...

Ingredients
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
Steps
  1. Cook bacon in pan until cooked to preference. Usually around 3 mins each side. Remove from pan, but leave grease
  2. Beat Eggs and milk together in a bowl, add to pan of bacon grease on med-high heat.
  3. scramble to hearts desire - once it looks about done, add cheese to melt as eggs are finishing
  4. Remove from pan and season to preference
