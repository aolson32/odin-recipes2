Bread

Bread worth Fighting For

This delightful bread is the pride of my House. The recipe has belonged to my family for quite a while.

I remember spending afternoons with my grandmother making this bread.

Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • water
Steps
  1. Add the yeast to warm water and let set for while completing the next step
  2. Scald the milk. Take off the heat and mix in the honey
  3. Once cooled enough to not kill the yeast, mix the water & yeast mixture with the milk & Honey mixture in a large bowl
  4. Add the salt and flour - mix until combined - let rest 10 mins
  5. Knead the dough for 10 mins, let rest until doubled in size
  6. Move to pans, let rest until doubled again
  7. Bake it
